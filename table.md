## Key Notes on Units and SourcesGDP per capita

- Nominal USD, typically chained or current dollars from BEA quarterly/annual releases (latest often Q3 2025 or 2024 full-year).
- Unemployment rate: Seasonally adjusted percent, monthly from BLS LAUS (e.g., December 2025 preliminary data shows many states lower than prior figures).
- Median household income: USD, from U.S. Census ACS 1-year estimates (2024 data released ~2025).
- Poverty rate: Percent of population below federal poverty line, Census ACS or CPS-based (2024 estimates).
- Life expectancy: Years at birth, CDC/NCHS vital statistics (2024 provisional/final data shows U.S. at ~79 years, state variations persist).
- High school & Bachelor's %: Percent of population 25+, U.S. Census ACS/CPS (recent 2024/2025 releases).
- Infant mortality rate: Deaths per 1,000 live births, CDC/NCHS linked birth/infant death files (2023 final ~5.61 national; state 2023/2024 provisional).
- Renewable energy %: Percent of total electricity generation from renewables (wind, solar, hydro, etc.), EIA data (2024 full-year or latest monthly).
- Obesity rate: Percent of adults with BMI ≥30, self-reported from CDC BRFSS (2024 data shows all states ≥25%, Midwest/South highest).
